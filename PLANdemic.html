About PLANdemic

As COVID-19 continues to induce turbulence in our lives, infrastructure and economies, India continues to show its resilience, strength and unity. However, despite best efforts, our daily lives, working patterns, regulatory and legal structures are constantly facing renewed challenges. PLANdemic is a case study competition where the brightest minds of the country come up with a solution for the recent challenges faced by the startups in the country. The event is aimed at bringing out the creative and marketing potential of the participants while balancing constraints at the same time.

Rules and Regulations

  1. The competition is open for all pan-India.

  2. The last date to submit the registration form will be 30th May 2020.

  3. The last date to submit the case study will be 10th June 2020.

  4. The Submission guidelines will be mentioned in the case study.

  5. Case study once submitted will be considered final.

  6. No case study will be accepted post-deadline & no changes will be allowed.
